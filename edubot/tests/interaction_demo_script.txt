EDUBOT INTERACTION DEMO SCRIPT
Version: 1.0
Date: 2026-04-07

MUC TIEU
- Chay demo thuc te de kiem tra cac flow chinh cua he thong:
  1) Tre day robot
  2) Tre hoi kien thuc trong-topic va ngoai-topic
  3) Quiz: dung/sai, giai thich, khen, cham diem
  4) Tro chuyen tu nhien
  5) Switch topic
  6) Fallback an toan khi cau hoi qua nang
  7) Xac nhan topic co du lieu

CHUAN BI
1) Chay app o che do interactive.
2) Nhap ten be va tuoi.
3) Neu co lenh status trong script, go chu status de check state.

============================================================
SESSION A (SPACE) - 10 den 15 phut
============================================================

A1) KHOI DONG + DAY CHU DE
Tre: Hom nay con muon hoc ve he mat troi.
Ky vong:
- Robot xac nhan chu de
- Robot moi tre day tiep

Tre: Mat Troi la ngoi sao o trung tam he mat troi.
Ky vong:
- Robot ack dung ngu canh space

Tre: Cac hanh tinh quay quanh Mat Troi.
Ky vong:
- Robot tiep tuc o TEACH, khong nhay sang animal

Tre: status
Ky vong:
- phase la teach

A2) HOI KIEN THUC TRONG-TOPIC (UU TIEN)
Tre: Tai sao co mua trong nam?
Ky vong:
- Tra loi dung huong space (lien quan do nghieng truc Trai Dat va chuyen dong)
- Co the moi tre hoi tiep hoac day tiep

Tre: Mat Trang co tu phat sang khong?
Ky vong:
- Robot tra loi dung (khong tu phat sang, phan xa anh sang)

A3) NGOAI TOPIC / CAU HOI NANG
Tre: Lo den thao tac thong tin luong tu nhu the nao?
Ky vong:
- Neu khong chac, robot fallback an toan
- Khong duoc tra loi kieu khang dinh manh nhu chac chan 100%

A4) TRO CHUYEN TU NHIEN
Tre: Hom nay con vui qua.
Ky vong:
- Robot dap lai tu nhien, than thien

Tre: Ban ten gi?
Ky vong:
- Robot tra loi giao tiep on
- Khong lam vo context hoc

A5) VAO QUIZ
Tre: Oke kiem tra di.
Ky vong:
- Chuyen sang QUIZ
- Robot dua cau hoi dau tien

Tre: status
Ky vong:
- phase la quiz

A6) TRA LOI DUNG
(Trong cau hoi quiz #1)
Tre: [Tra loi dung theo dap an robot da hoi]
Ky vong:
- Robot khen
- score tang
- correct tang

A7) TRA LOI SAI / KHONG BIET + GIAI THICH
(Trong cau hoi quiz #2)
Tre: khong biet
Ky vong:
- Robot dua dap an + giai thich ngan
- Robot hoi da hieu chua

Tre: chua hieu
Ky vong:
- Robot giai thich lan 2 de hon
- Sau do chuyen cau tiep theo nhe nhang

A8) QUIZ CONTROL
Tre: hoi lai di
Ky vong:
- Robot lap lai cau hien tai

Tre: cau khac di
Ky vong:
- Robot bo qua cau hien tai, hoi cau moi

A9) KET THUC SESSION
Tiep tuc den het QUIZ_TOTAL cau.
Tre: status
Ky vong:
- Robot ve reward, tong ket diem
- Session ket thuc ve idle/end


============================================================
SESSION B (ANIMAL) - 8 den 12 phut
============================================================

B1) SWITCH TOPIC
Tre: Minh doi chu de nhe, hoc ve dong vat di.
Ky vong:
- Robot xac nhan doi topic
- Reset focus sang animal

B2) DAY + HOI TRONG ANIMAL
Tre: Con trung la dong vat nho, co 6 chan.
Ky vong:
- Robot ack dung context animal

Tre: Chuoi thuc an la gi?
Ky vong:
- Robot tra loi dung noi dung animal

B3) KIEM TRA LEAK DOMAIN
Tre: Sao choi la gi?
Ky vong:
- Neu dang khoa animal, uu tien dung context hien tai
- Neu robot tra loi space thi van phai on dinh va khong vo state

B4) QUIZ NHANH
Tre: Oke kiem tra di.
Ky vong:
- Vao quiz

Tra loi 1 cau dung, 1 cau sai.
Ky vong:
- Dung: khen
- Sai: giai thich


============================================================
PASS/FAIL RUBRIC (NHANH)
============================================================

PASS NEU DAT TOI THIEU 8/10 TIEU CHI:
1) Topic capture dung o dau session
2) In-topic QA tra loi hop ly
3) Out-of-topic nang co fallback an toan
4) Co the chat tu nhien ma khong vo state
5) Switch topic duoc xac nhan ro rang
6) Vao quiz duoc khi tre opt-in
7) Co feedback khac nhau cho dung va sai
8) Co explain loop khi tre noi chua hieu
9) score/correct thay doi dung theo quiz
10) Session ket thuc reward on dinh

FAIL NEU CO BAT KY DIEM NAO SAU DAY:
- Hallucination nang (tra loi chac chan sai cho cau nang)
- Mat state lien tuc (phase nhay loan)
- Quiz khong cham diem hoac khong ket thuc
- Switch topic khong hoat dong
