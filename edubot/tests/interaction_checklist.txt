SCENARIO 1 - CHILD TEACHES ROBOT ABOUT A TOPIC
Goal: Verify TEACH phase and topic capture.
Child says:
- Hôm nay tớ muốn dạy cho cậu biết về chủ đề hệ mặt trời
- Mat Troi la ngoi sao o trung tam he mat troi.
Expected:
- Robot acknowledges topic and invites teaching.
- Robot keeps context in same domain.
Observe:
- status shows phase=teach.

SCENARIO 2 - CHILD ASKS KNOWLEDGE (IN-TOPIC FIRST)
Goal: Verify in-topic retrieval priority.
Child says:
- Tai sao co mua trong nam?
Expected:
- Robot answers with domain-consistent explanation.
- No abrupt topic jump to animal domain.
Observe:
- Follow-up invites either next question or continue teaching.

SCENARIO 3 - CHILD ASKS OUT-OF-TOPIC / ADVANCED QUESTION
Goal: Verify uncertain fallback behavior.
Child says:
- Lo den co thong tin luong tu nhu the nao?
Expected:
- If model is not sure, response should fall back safely.
- Final safe phrase should avoid hallucinated certainty.
Observe:
- System should not invent high-confidence facts.

SCENARIO 4 - QUIZ FLOW, CORRECT VS WRONG ANSWER, EXPLANATION
Goal: Verify quiz lifecycle and answer evaluation.
Steps:
1) Teach 3-4 turns until robot offers a quiz.
2) Child opts in: oke kiem tra di.
3) For one question answer correctly.
4) For next question answer: khong biet.
Expected:
- Correct answer: praise + score update.
- Wrong/uncertain: explanation shown, then asks if child understood.
- If child says chua hieu: explain again in simpler style.
Observe:
- status reflects quiz progress.
- quiz_result events update score/correct.
